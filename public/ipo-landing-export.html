Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our IPO alerts

What is InvestorStream? ▼
InvestorStream is a free IPO intelligence platform that provides real-time alerts, market analysis, and comprehensive data on upcoming initial public offerings. We help investors stay informed about pre-IPO companies and make better investment decisions.
Is the service really free? ▼
Yes! Our basic alerts and IPO tracking are completely free. We offer premium features for advanced investors, but you can access essential IPO intelligence without any cost or credit card required.
How do I receive alerts? ▼
After signing up with your email, you'll receive alerts directly to your inbox. You can customize which IPOs to track and what types of alerts you want to receive (filings, price changes, news, etc.).
What companies do you track? ▼
We track major pre-IPO companies across all sectors including tech giants like SpaceX, Stripe, and OpenAI, as well as emerging companies in fintech, healthcare, and more. Our database includes both US and international IPO candidates.
Is this financial advice? ▼
No. InvestorStream provides educational information and market intelligence only. We are not registered investment advisors. Always consult with a qualified financial professional before making investment decisions.
